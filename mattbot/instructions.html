Getting Started

Welcome to the comprehensive instruction guide! This documentation will walk you through every step of creating your own autonomous mobile robot, from initial construction to advanced operation.

The process is divided into three main phases: Construction (building the physical robot), Setup (configuring software and sensors), and Operation (running and controlling the robot). Each phase builds upon the previous one, so it's recommended to follow them in order.

These guides provide detailed instructions, troubleshooting tips, and best practices for building and using the autonomous mobile robot.

Prerequisites & Requirements

Tools Required

Soldering iron, screwdrivers (Phillips Head, Flat Head, Hex), 3D printer access, laser cutter access, multimeter, computer

Skill Level

Intermediate electronics knowledge, basic programming experience recommended

Budget

Approximately $800-1200 depending on component choices and sourcing
